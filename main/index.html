Wooden Ships Overview

Wooden Ships explores European Shipping activity and observations from 1750 to 1850. Filter the map by country to better understand varying shipping patterns by colonial powers. Hover over the ships to view individual log entries to read weather observations as well as candid events on board from the captains! Select ships with particular weather or climatic features, such as temperature recordings or encounters with sea ice. Check out wind speed patterns across the Atlantic. The data consists of logbook entries written by the captain of each ship.

Data sources: Climatological Database for the World's Oceans 1750-1850 for digitized shipping logs, Natural Earth for line work, and The Noun Project for icons.

Cartographers/developers/designers/shipping enthusiasts: Scott Farley, Starr Moss, and Meghan Kelly.
